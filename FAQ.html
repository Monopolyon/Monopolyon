Rules for using the site
How to add your own area
ᐯ

     To add your own area, you will need a MetaMask wallet in which the BSC network will be added. You can read how to install the MetaMask wallet in this article. Adding the BSC network description here.

     If the amount of MNP in your wallet meets the set requirement (equal to 21000 or more). You can add your area for display on the main screen. To do this, click on the gear icon, which is located in the upper right corner of the screen, next to the wallet connection button. You will go to the settings page. To save data in the blockchain, you must have a MetaMask wallet installed. Click the Connect MetaMask button and log into your wallet. After logging in, you will see the address of your wallet and the MNP balance on it. Now click the Save Setting button and confirm your transaction in MetaMask. After the transaction is confirmed within 10 minutes, your area will be added to the main screen. To find out how to enter additional data, such as Email, Name, Motto, Link, and add a Logo to your area, read below.

What is additional data
ᐯ

     Email, Name, Motto, Link

     You can add additional data that will be displayed when you hover over your area.

     If you add a link, then when you click on your area, the user will go to the specified link.

     Be careful when adding data, they must strictly meet the requirements of the site.

Email - must meet general standards (example@mail.com). Length no more than 100 characters.

Name - maximum length 30 characters.

Motto - maximum length 80 characters.

Link - must start with http:// or https:// maximum length 512 characters.

If some data does not meet the requirements, you will see a hint.

What are display settings
ᐯ

Background Color and Flex Logo

     Given that areas dynamically adapt to the size of the display, they can take different forms (square, horizontal or vertical rectangle).

     Background Color - sets the background color if the Logo does not cover the entire area. It makes sense to adjust only if the Flex Logo parameter is OFF.

     Flex Logo - when the parameter is turned on, your logo will always stretch to the full width and height of the area (without maintaining proportions).

     To configure the Background Color and Flex Logo parameters, go to the Display Setting tab.

How to add your own logo
ᐯ

     On the Display Setting tab, you can add a URL logo that will be displayed in your area.

     Please note that the URL must meet certain requirements for correct display, namely:
     Must start with http:// or https:// length should not exceed 512 characters the logo must be in one of the following formats SVG, PNG, JPG, WEBP, JPEG, GIF

     If the Logo by the link becomes unavailable, then instead of it, the standard NO IMG picture is displayed.

     If the Logo format does not meet the requirements, the Logo will not be displayed, instead of it, the standard NO IMG picture will be displayed.
