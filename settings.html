Important
  • On the Display Settings tab, you can add a Logo URL and make its display settings including background color and flex logo.
  • If you want to update your data, you need to fill in all the fields again as the data is not partially updated but is completely overwritten, including in the Display Settings tab.
Data Settings Desctiption
  • You can register a wallet by leaving all fields empty. Then when you hover over your area, only the address and balance of the wallet will be displayed. When you click on it, there will be no transition, and instead of the logo, a standard picture will be displayed.
  • Email:  Here you can specify your email that all users will see
  • Name:  Here you can specify the name of your company or any other information
  • Motto:  Here you can specify the slogan or motto of your company or any other information
  • Link:  Here you can specify a link that will be followed when you click on your area
  • *:  Fill in the fields according to the specified number of characters. Otherwise, the specified information will not be displayed
Display Settings Description
  • Background Color:  Fills your area with the selected color if the logo does not cover the entire width or height of the area. It makes sense to apply only if Flex Logo OFF
  • Flex Logo:  Stretches the Logo to the full height and width of your area (Logo proportions are not maintained)
  • Logo URL:  Link to the logo. Please note that only certain logo formats are supported, namely SVG, PNG, JPG, WEBP, JPEG, GIF. The total length of the link should not exceed 512 characters. If the logo format does not meet the requirement, the logo will not be displayed. Instead, the standard No IMG picture will be displayed. If the logo by Url becomes unavailable, the standard No IMG picture will be displayed.
  • *:  By hovering over the areas at the bottom, you can see a pop-up window with your data
  • **:  If a Link value is set, when you click on the area, you will be redirected to the specified link
History Description
  • *:  On this page, all the settings that this wallet has made are displayed
At the moment, the settings page is only available in desktop browsers. We apologize for any inconvenience caused.
